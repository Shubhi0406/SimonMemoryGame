HOW TO PLAY

Simon will give the first signal. (One of the Color Lenses will light up, accompanied by a tone.)
• Repeat that signal by touching the same Color Lens.
• Simon will then add another color to the sequence by lighting up a Color Lens.
• Repeat the first signal and then the newly added signal.
• Simon will continue to add a color to the sequence.
Continue to play the sequence for as long as you can!

• If you fail to complete the sequence in the correct order, the Buzzer will sound and the game will end.
• Simon records your High Score so you can play to beat it.
